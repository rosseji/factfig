We meet a great guy, Nick, who is based out of Monash Uni. He’s made two cool packages that deal with missing data!

narnia

devtools::install_github("njtierney/narnia")
library(narnia)

and

visdat

devtools::install_github("njtierney/visdat")
library(visdat)

Might be useful for things like “HoursWorked” in H.
